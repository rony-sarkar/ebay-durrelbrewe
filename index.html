Regal Ground White Pepper, Spice, Seasoning

  • Finely ground consistency
  • Perfect for lightly colored foods
  • Slightly less pungent than black pepper
  • Great value, multi-purpose ingredient

Beige in color, ground white pepper has a refined flavor and aroma with a mildly hot taste. While this seasoning is closely related to black pepper, it has a smoother skin and slightly less pungent taste. Since ground white pepper has a lighter coloring, it’s ideal for light-colored foods, like mashed potatoes, cauliflower, gravies, and creamy sauces. Provides you with plenty of spice for your savory appetizers, sides, and entrees.

In order to save you time during food prep, this white pepper is pre-ground. This consistency makes it easy to mix in with other ingredients and sprinkle on top of your finished entrees. Ground pepper also offers a more consistent flavor than whole peppercorns.

White pepper has a flavor that's similar to black pepper, but slightly more mild. Because of this, keep in mind that the two are not interchangeable in your recipes.

Due to its subtle coloring, white pepper is often used in light-colored foods such as alfredo, bechamel sauces, mashed potatoes, eggs, and cauliflower.
